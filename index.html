About the server

ImmaFreedom is an associated server of TotalFreedom - The original Free OP Server! We have professional volunteer staff devoted to ensuring a safe, free and friendly community for all ages! Our plugins ensure you're never bored of the same old. We also host custom events, solely dedicated to providing a unique and intriguing experience for all members. Give us a shot, and connect today: play.immafreedom.eu
